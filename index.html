About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Embedded Systems Group at TU Delft, supervised by Prof. Dr. Koen Langendoen and Dr. Marco Zuniga Zamalloa.

My current research focuses on using radio-frequency sensing to estimate human behavior for privacy-preserving human-robot interaction. I am interested in sensing systems that can support smooth, responsive interaction without relying on visually intrusive perception.

Before joining TU Delft, I received an MSc in Information Technology from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology and a BSc in Computing from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. During that period, I worked on wireless network systems, especially BLE-based sensing and localization.
